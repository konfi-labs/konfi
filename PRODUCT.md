# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Print-shop staff, order managers, and production planners who use the admin dashboard to track, prioritize, and fulfill configurable printing orders. They work in a task-driven context where speed and accuracy matter more than visual delight.

## Product Purpose

Konfi admin is the operational control center for configurable printing and e-commerce workflows. It lets staff manage orders, monitor production status, coordinate fulfillment, and configure catalog data. Success means the tool disappears into the task: users find what they need, act on it, and move on.

## Brand Personality

Professional, efficient, calm.

The admin app should feel like a trusted workspace: confident but not loud, dense but not cluttered, responsive but not restless.

## Anti-references

- Cluttered dashboards with competing bright accents and decorative charts.
- Overly decorative SaaS templates (gradient text, glassmorphism, hero-metric cards).
- Loud primary colors used for decoration rather than action/selection.
- Heavy motion that delays interaction or draws attention away from data.
- Inconsistent component vocabulary across screens.

## Design Principles

1. **Efficiency first.** Every element should earn its place by helping a user complete a task faster or with fewer errors.
2. **Calm density.** Information can be dense, but it must be organized and breathable; noise is the enemy.
3. **Semantic clarity.** Color, weight, and spacing communicate state and hierarchy, not brand enthusiasm.
4. **Consistent affordances.** Buttons, menus, tables, and form controls behave the same way across the admin surface.
5. **Restrained emphasis.** Use accent color for primary actions, current selection, and status indicators only.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Target WCAG 2.1 AA for contrast, focus visibility, and interactive target sizes.
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`: keep motion functional and provide instant alternatives.
- Support keyboard navigation and screen-reader semantics for tables, dialogs, and form controls.
